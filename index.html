AccompanyHer

Connect with members of your community to help or get help with challenging daily tasks related to women's health and safety, from accompaniment to a Planned Parenthood swarming with protesters to picking up Plan B from the local drugstore.

Learn more

About Us

AccomanyHer helps make health care more accessible, especially in an increasingly hostile political environment for people seeking women's health essentials like birth control or access to Planned Parenthood.

Travel safely

Picketers in front of your local Planned Parenthood? Need to get to the train station after dark? With AccomanyHer, find someone to keep you safe.

Community connections

Connect and converse with kind and empathetic members of your community over coffee or drinks.

Pick up medication

Don't worry about being judged for your age or appearance while at the pharmacy and have someone else pick up your contraception for you.

Sign up to help out

Are you able to help out your community members by providing some small service? Fill out the form below to sign up! Once you've signed up, people seeking assistance will be able to reach out to you. Don't worry, we'll make sure your personal information isn't freely available on the Internet -- only your first name, ZIP code, and available services will be visible.

Request a service

Need help with something? Look through our database for people near you who can help. When you've found someone you'd like to contact, click the contact button and send them a message with your contact information so you can coordinate.
